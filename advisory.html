Engagements · 02

Four formats. Each calibrated to where a specific decision sits.

01
Education

Team Education.

Half-day or full-day workshops on how the systems your team buys from actually work.

Built from the inside of those systems, not from vendor documentation. Aimed at commercial and marketing teams who need to operate confidently in a market where the platforms set most of the rules. The session is closed-room, not branded, and it is not a sales pitch for anything.

What you receive
  • Half-day or full-day format
  • Custom-built to your stack and team
  • Pre-session brief and post-session reading list
When it fits

"Use when the activation team can run the platforms but cannot defend the choices in a procurement meeting."

02
Advisory

Executive Advisory.

A standing retainer for leadership teams that want an unconflicted outside view.

Regular briefings, pressure-testing of strategy, on-call analysis when the structure of a market shifts. Calibrated to where the actual decisions sit. Not a deliverable schedule. A relationship that survives the moments the agency cannot tell you what they really think.

What you receive
  • Quarterly leadership briefing
  • Monthly written market read
  • On-call async access between sessions
When it fits

"Use when the board is asking questions the executive team cannot fully answer without a vendor in the room."

03
Market

Market Entry & Expansion.

Independent ground truth for technology companies entering or expanding in ANZ.

For platforms outside the region considering entry, and for those who have launched and want to sense-check their commercial strategy before committing further capital. The work is structural. Who buys, who decides, where the gravitational centres are, and what the market will not accept.

What you receive
  • Market structure read on the relevant category
  • Buy-side decisioning map
  • Commercial strategy review
When it fits

"Use before the regional commitment is made, or before doubling down on a strategy that has not produced the expected outcome."

04
Transaction

Transaction Advisory.

An operator's read on the asset — separate from the deal advisor's incentives.

Diligence on assets in programmatic, marketing technology, retail media or measurement. Independent from any party in the deal. The work is technical and structural, not financial. The aim is to make sure the asset is what the seller says it is, and that the thesis is what the market will support.

What you receive
  • Technical and structural diligence
  • Competitive position read
  • Risk register against the investment thesis
When it fits

"Use when the deal team needs an independent operator's read separate from the deal advisor's incentives."

How an engagement works · 03

Four steps. No surprises. No locked-in vendor recommendation at the end.

  1. 01

    Scoping call

    30 minutes. We agree what is in scope, what is not, and whether the engagement is the right starting point. No commitment.

  2. 02

    Pre-work

    A short questionnaire and a request for the artefacts already in your stack. The work begins before the first meeting.

  3. 03

    Working sessions

    Closed-room. Calibrated to the people who actually decide. Documented, but not dressed-up.

  4. 04

    Findings & ownership

    You own the document, the framework and the analysis. There is no follow-on quote, no implementation upsell.
