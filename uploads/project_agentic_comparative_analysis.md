## Known Accuracy Issues

**Gemini date error (critical):** Gemini dates the TTD-Publicis dispute as "late 2024 and early 2025." This is wrong. The FirmDecisions audit and Publicis client memo are dated March 2026, confirmed across GPT, KIMI, Andrew's file, and underlying Adweek/Ad Age sources. Always use the 2026 date. All Gemini date claims should be treated with caution; other Gemini content is generally usable.

**KIMI Walmart/Sparky conflation:** KIMI states "Walmart partnered with OpenAI to sell products via ChatGPT's Sparky assistant." This conflates Walmart's own Sparky (consumer-facing assistant) with OpenAI's Instant Checkout. Verify before using KIMI's framing.

## Claims to Verify Before Citing

**Butler/Till + Clubtails outcome metrics** (KIMI, DeepSeek): 82% supply chain cost reduction, 40% impression lift, 30% CPM drop, 98% VCR, <1% MFA, 80% above DV benchmarks. Two sources give the same triple — positive signal but both may reference the same press release. Verify against original PubMatic Dec 2025 announcement.

**PubMatic "250 agentic deals" + USD 282.9m FY25 revenue** (DeepSeek, KIMI): Verify against PubMatic SEC filings.

**IMAA "180 members, 30-40% of ad spend, >50% of digital"** (GPT, Gemini): Explicitly flagged [uncertain] — check IMAA's own website.

**Mutinex AUD 132.5m valuation** (Perplexity): No other source confirms. Verify.

**Mutinex Open MMM Validation Framework date:** DeepSeek says July 2025, GPT says July 2025, Andrew's file says 16 September 2025 for formal NA launch. Likely both true (published mid-2025, formally launched NA in September). Verify before citing a specific date.

**Gemini-only: Westpac, Telstra, Suncorp** experimenting with agentic AI to bridge channel-based structure and non-linear consumer journey. No other source names these companies. Verify via direct vendor or trade press before citing.

**Gemini-only: Sellforte "miROAS" and "The Missing 24%"** research paper. Not surfaced elsewhere. Verify against Sellforte's published material.

**KIMI: Amazon DSP fees as low as 1%** for major spending clients. Directionally consistent across sources but the 1% figure is uncertain.

**Adobe Analytics 1,200%/4,700% YoY AI traffic to US retail sites** (DeepSeek): Large numbers off small bases. Cite with absolute traffic context if available.

**Bain "80% rely on zero-click in 40% of searches"** (DeepSeek): Verify against underlying Bain study.

**Walmart-OpenAI Instant Checkout 3x lower conversion** (GPT): Read the original Wired piece directly — it's the strongest counter-narrative in the corpus.

**Mars United "ANZ retail media AUD 1.6bn to 2.8bn by 2027"** vs **PwC ">AUD 3bn by 2027"**: Both are forecasts; cite both and flag the spread.
