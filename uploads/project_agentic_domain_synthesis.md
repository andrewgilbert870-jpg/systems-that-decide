Domain-by-domain synthesis for the "Systems That Decide" Substack series. Use this when drafting domain-specific content to identify which sources contributed what and where conflicts remain.

**Why:** Each AI research tool has a different shape — Perplexity is strongest on technical depth, Gemini on frameworks, GPT on commercial reality-checks, DeepSeek on vendor quantitative data, KIMI on outcome metrics, Andrew's file on ANZ regulatory and broadcaster-OOH.

**How to apply:** When drafting a domain, use this file to surface the distinctive claims from each source before writing, and cross-reference project_agentic_comparative_analysis.md for what needs verification.

## DOMAIN 1: INFRASTRUCTURE

**Convergent picture:** Six of seven sources agree. Agentic buying is goal-oriented multi-step orchestration — structurally distinct from GenAI (content production) and traditional programmatic (rule-based RTB). Most of what is live today is "co-pilot or orchestration layer, not a free-running bidder" (Elicit, GPT, Perplexity use this exact framing).

**Perplexity distinctive:** Most technical. ARTF v1.0 as "OpenRTB Patch protocol" mutating bid requests under host control. AdCP task names (`create_media_buy`, `sync_creatives`). LiveRamp UCP: embedding-based identity exchange at sub-100ms latency. 142+ citations. IAB Australia showing programmatic losing share to IO during 2025 sport tentpoles. Australian internet ad market AUD 18.4bn in calendar 2025, +11.5% YoY.

**Gemini distinctive:** Cleanest taxonomy. Five-protocol comparison table (MCP/AdCP/AAMP/UCP/ACP). Three-tier system-comparison table (Traditional Programmatic vs GenAI vs Agentic AI). AAMP three-pillar structure (Agent Foundations, Agentic Protocols, Trust and Transparency). "Embedded Human Judgment" as AdCP feature — now verified correct. ANZ: Joanna Georges as Scope3 ANZ leadership (formerly TTD ANZ) — the only named ANZ-based agentic-infrastructure executive in the corpus. Argues ANZ retail concentration creates structural incentive for "Agentic Direct" protocols at Nine, Seven, News Corp.

**DeepSeek distinctive:** PubMatic AgenticOS: 250+ agentic deals fiscal 2025, USD 282.9m FY25 revenue, 30x more compute demand (verify SEC filings). AWS RTB Fabric (Amazon private network for RTB workloads, ms latency, up to 80% lower network cost) — no other source covered this. Programmatic waste estimated at 55% of total spend.

**GPT distinctive:** Clearest articulation that what's live is "assisted workflow automation, not fully autonomous market-making." ANA Q1 2025 Programmatic Transparency Benchmark: 41% of programmatic budgets to "effective ad impressions," 37.8% optimisation gap, USD 21.6bn potential efficiency.

**KIMI distinctive:** Butler/Till Clubtails outcome metrics (82% supply chain cost reduction, 40% impression lift, 30% CPM drop, 98% VCR, <1% MFA, 80% above DV benchmarks) — verify before citing.

**Elicit distinctive:** Seri 2025 "Agentic Ads" paper (International Symposium on Embedded Computing): specialist agents for multimodal product understanding, policy/consent enforcement, constrained bidding, fairness-aware exposure, auditor-ready explanations. Academic anchor only.

**Andrew's file distinctive:** IAB Tech Lab Agent Registry going live 3 March 2026, 10 entries by 11 March. PMG Alli AAMP integration April 2026. AdRoll-PubMatic cross-platform MCP diagnostics 23 April 2026. Scope3 staff cuts early 2026 ("agentic pivot collided with revenue reality") — credibility-of-stewardship signal no AI source surfaced.

**Key conflict:** AAMP date — some sources use late 2025 framing; the official announcement was 26 Feb 2026, framework published 16 March 2026.

**Biggest gap:** Walled-garden interoperability. No source documents Meta, Google, Amazon, or TikTok committing natively to AdCP or AAMP through May 2026. Amazon MCP is the first crack.

## DOMAIN 2: BRANDS

**Convergent picture:** Six sources agree on AEO/GEO as real but immature disciplines. Six agree on the Aggarwal et al. Princeton paper as academic foundation. Two protocols define the frame: ACP (OpenAI+Stripe) and UCP (Google). Product data readiness is the foundational requirement.

**GPT distinctive:** Walmart-OpenAI Instant Checkout: 3x lower conversion than website redirect (Wired). The single most important counter-narrative in the corpus. Target March 2026 terms update making AI-agent-authorised purchases binding on consumers.

**Gemini distinctive:** 6-dimension GEO framework: WHO, WHEN, WHERE, WHY, WHAT, AI Retrievability. Structured-catalog data table (GTIN/MPN, JSON-LD, FAQ/HowTo, Review Aggregate). "Ecommerce AIO" as new umbrella term. Westpac, Telstra, Suncorp as Australian brands experimenting with agentic AI to bridge channel-based structure and non-linear journey — only ANZ brand-side experimentation thread in any source (verify before citing).

**Perplexity distinctive:** Visa-YouGov "State of Digital Commerce in Asia Pacific 2025" — 14,000 consumers, 14 markets. 74% use AI for discovery, 32% reluctant to share personal data, 45% want stronger payment security evidence. Strongest APAC consumer dataset in corpus. ANZ: 49% of Australians using generative AI tools, 65% of global Google searches ending without a click.

**DeepSeek distinctive:** Salesforce 2025 Connected Shoppers (39% use AI for discovery, majority of Gen Z). Lily AI/Bain: 40% use AI for shopping. Adobe Analytics: AI-source traffic to US retail sites up 1,200% YoY March 2025, 4,700% by July. ReFiBuy Commerce Intelligence Engine launched September 2025 (Steve Madden case study).

**KIMI distinctive:** ChatGPT 800m weekly users, Gemini 750m monthly, Google AI Overviews in 16% of all searches. 40-60% of cited AI sources change month-to-month. Shopify AI-driven orders grew 15x in 2025. EMARKETER: 31.3% of US population will use generative AI search in 2026.

**Andrew's file distinctive:** Regulatory layer — Privacy Act ADM rule Dec 2026, Children's Online Privacy Code by 10 Dec 2026, IAB AI Transparency Framework 15 Jan 2026. Azoma Agentic Merchant Protocol 12 March 2026 (Mars, L'Oréal, Unilever, Beiersdorf, Reckitt as early adopters). Edelman 2026 Trust Barometer: 37% cite generative AI growth as top driver of declining trust. Quad April 2026: 75% of US consumers would lose trust if AI recommendations were sponsored.

**Key conflict:** AEO/GEO maturity — Perplexity/GPT/Gemini frame as immature-but-emerging; DeepSeek/KIMI treat as more established. Best read: productised by vendors, but empirical evidence base is thin.

**ANZ key data (cross-source):** Bunnings Buddy = Australia's first consumer-facing agentic shopping assistant. Time Under Tension: 79% task completion across 11 ANZ retailers. ANZ DTC brands (Who Gives A Crap, Koala, Eucalyptus, Aje, Cotton On) absent from public agentic-protocol announcements.

## DOMAIN 3: AGENCIES

**Convergent picture:** Agency value chain unbundling in slow motion. Six sources agree on the holdco AI race shape and the TTD-Publicis dispute as a structural moment. What's compressed by agents: glue work between functions. What retains value: strategy, creative origination, complex orchestration, audit, vendor leverage.

**Gemini distinctive:** Holdco-versus-platform operating margin frame: Meta/Google/TTD at 30-42% vs holdcos at 12-18%. The cleanest explanation for why TTD-Publicis is structurally a margin war, not just a transparency dispute. Three-party position table (Publicis: cites failed audit, motivated by margin protection; TTD: denies audit failure, motivated by direct brand expansion; WPP/Dentsu: scaling back OpenPath, motivated by principal-media position concerns). Note: Gemini dates the dispute "late 2024 and early 2025" — wrong, it is March 2026.

**DeepSeek distinctive:** Financial divergence: Publicis posted strong gains through Q1-Q3 2025 (Coca-Cola NA media, Mars global, PayPal global, Paramount global won). WPP pre-tax profits down 71% H1 2025, revenue shrinking. eMarketer: WPP's decline "exemplifies vulnerability in the traditional model."

**Perplexity distinctive:** WPP removed from FTSE 100 after sharp valuation decline. XPON joint report with Independent Media Agencies of NZ on indie agencies reshaping growth models around AI.

**GPT distinctive:** Agencies face three options when AI compresses execution labour: (1) accept lower fee pools, (2) shift to higher-value advisory/orchestration, (3) repackage automation as proprietary value. WPP Open Pro framing as "agency disintermediation by an agency itself."

**KIMI distinctive:** Forrester's Jay Pattisall: agencies moving from "bespoke alchemy" to "operating model." Publicis represents >10% of TTD gross billings (Morningstar estimate).

**Andrew's file distinctive:** Omnicom-IPG synergy specifics. WPP Production (Richard Glasson, 23 Feb 2026). Dentsu JPY 26bn 2026 restructuring (JPY 12bn for AI/data). S4 Capital H1 2025 revenue -12.7%, headcount -8.9%. ANA 2026 principal media study (58% used principal-based buying, 90% concerned, 57% have written guidelines). Microsoft-Publicis partnership expansion 8 April 2026 = most concrete agentic-marketing alliance in market.

**ANZ agency specifics:** Publicis acquired Atomic 212 (Jan 2025) as CoreAI ANZ vehicle. Won Microsoft account. Surviving independents: Hatched, Half Dome, Bohemia, Murmur, Ryvalmedia, AKQA (WPP). Seven/SCA merger (Jan 2026) concentrates national TV+audio supply. Foxtel/DAZN as second major sports+streaming counterparty. No Australian independent has announced an agentic-buying pilot at the scale of Butler/Till.

## DOMAIN 4: DSPs AND SUPPLY STACK

**Convergent picture:** Five major DSPs and four major SSPs now agentic-named. TTD transparency crisis real and material across six of seven sources (Elicit thin). Walled-garden bridge = largest unresolved question.

**Gemini distinctive:** Structural framing: protocol ownership war = whoever wins the standards politics shapes who collects protocol-level rents. Financial-markets regulatory precedent: if an entity controls infrastructure through which trades execute, it cannot simultaneously trade its own book — the cleanest analogy for the AdCP/AAMP conflict. Cartology/Coles 360/Wesfarmers-Flybuys ANZ retail media table: the most useful local-market mapping in any source. Cartology repositioning "from FMCG-direct to wooing agencies." Coles 360 launched "Impact" suite. Wesfarmers "weaponising loyalty data from Kmart, Target, Bunnings, Catch" for cross-brand agentic targeting.

**DeepSeek distinctive:** PubMatic AgenticOS commercial scale: 250+ deals, USD 282.9m FY25 revenue, NVIDIA GPU (30x more compute demand). Magnite Anoki ContextIQ integration. Mars United Report Card: 17 new capabilities across ANZ retail media networks in 2025. ANZ programmatic market: USD 441m in 2024, projected 23.55% CAGR to USD 2.96bn by 2033.

**KIMI distinctive:** Strongest documented outcome metrics (Butler/Till Clubtails). Amazon DSP Q4 2025 revenue growth 23% YoY vs TTD 14%. Amazon DSP fees as low as 1% for major clients (uncertain on the 1% figure).

**GPT distinctive:** SSP repositioning: "agentic buying threatens to collapse some intermediation layers while creating new sell-side control points." Equativ AdCP framing.

**Perplexity distinctive:** Most APAC-relevant SSP context. Magnite APAC presence (Japan, India strength). Untapped Growth Collective as PubMatic AgenticOS independent-agency partner.

**Andrew's file distinctive:** Most current dated launches. Walmart Connect: Marty (advertiser-facing, GA H1 2026) + Sparky (consumer-facing, ad formats piloting from fall 2025, 81% of users checking availability/specs before purchase). Walmart Connect FY24 USD 4.4bn, +27% YoY. Cartology ~AUD 750m (Morgan Stanley). TTD stock decline figures (~40% YTD 2026, 68% in 2025, 63% trailing 12 months). FirmDecisions named as the auditor.

**Biggest gap:** No source provides clean measured ROI on a production-grade agentic campaign at scale. All current outcome data is either small pilots, demoware, or vendor-disclosed. The Substack series should treat current outcome data as directional, not load-bearing.

## DOMAIN 5: MEASUREMENT

**Convergent picture:** Five sources frame consistently — legacy attribution broken before agents, agents make it worse. MMM renaissance: quarterly to always-on in 24 months. Hershey-Mutinex = the case study. Data quality = binding constraint. "Agents amplify bad data."

**DeepSeek distinctive:** Mutinex internal data: 75 engineers, 387,856 external data points monitored, GrowthOS 7.5 users/customer/month at 134-minute average session. DataMAITE launched June 2025, 95% labelling time reduction. Leadership change: Danny Bass departed, John Sintras returned to lead US/Multinational. Sarah Martinez (Tracer CCO): "Most companies don't have an AI problem. They have a data readiness problem." Gartner: poor data quality costs organisations avg USD 12.9m/year.

**Perplexity distinctive:** IAB Australia September 2025 MMM Landscape Report profiling 12 active vendors. Mutinex valuation ~AUD 132.5m (late-2024 capital raise). Mutinex >150% YoY growth and doubled quarterly customer wins (Mediaweek December 2025).

**Gemini distinctive:** "Marginal Incremental ROAS (miROAS)" as Sellforte's named optimisation metric. Sellforte Labs "The Missing 24%" research: MMMs excluding promotion data behave like last-click attribution, favour bottom-of-funnel channels. Traditional MMM vs Agentic MMM comparison table (update frequency, granularity, etc.).

**KIMI distinctive:** Precise Hershey-Mutinex framing. Aravind Chandrasekharan (TTD SVP engineering): "scale doesn't correct the gaps; it amplifies them." Sellforte company size: ~36 employees, ~USD 3m revenue, Finnish SaaS.

**Elicit distinctive:** Mittal 2025 and Hryshchanka 2025 academic papers extending MMM with Markov attribution and geo-holdout/user experiments for causal verification. Most consistent technical story: agentic systems being built around causality, incrementality, governance — not just prediction.

**Andrew's file distinctive:** Lifesight Mia launched 24 March 2026. Meridian Scenario Planner UI 19 Feb 2026. Sellforte Media Buyer Agent launched Q1 2026 with explicit capability to push bid changes directly into Google/Meta/TikTok in self-drive or assisted-driving mode. Privacy Sandbox formally abandoned. Adelaide AU integrated into Nielsen ONE from October 2025 — 2025 outcomes: 33% upper-funnel lift, 53% lower-funnel lift across attention-powered campaigns. Ehrenberg-Bass Institute (UniSA) as institutional influence on ANZ effectiveness culture.

**Biggest measurement gap:** Incrementality testing under agent autonomy. Six of seven sources flag the conceptual problem (geo holdouts contaminate when agents continuously optimise). None proposes a robust solution. Honest practitioner answer: policy-level validation (auditing the agent's optimisation policy), not decision-level testing.

**Mutinex Open MMM Validation Framework date conflict:** DeepSeek says July 2025, GPT says July 2025, Andrew's file says 16 September 2025 for formal NA launch. Probable reconciliation: framework published mid-2025, formally launched in North America September 2025. Verify before citing a specific date.

**ANZ measurement:** Australian measurement market mainstreamed MMM ahead of US peers in cadence and operating discipline. But ANZ is behind Europe and the US on integration of MMM into agentic buying loops (Sellforte pushing harder in Europe, Meridian ecosystem in US). Mutinex unified ANZ client list (across all sources): Domino's, Samsung, Lion, Asahi, Optus, SEEK, ING, TPG, REA Group, Honda, Freedom, Lendi/Aussie Home Loans, endota, One NZ, Bupa, plus Hershey internationally.
